How to play and earn rewards

Welcome to our Server!

This server features a unique PvP experience with special rewards for active players. Learn how to earn tokens and climb the ranks!

How to Earn Tokens

  • Eliminations: Earn 10 tokens per player kill
  • Airdrops: Capture airdrops to earn 50-100 tokens
  • Events: Participate in server events for bonus rewards
  • Red Zones: Survive and fight in red zones for double tokens

Server Meta

The current meta favors aggressive gameplay with tactical positioning. Use vehicles to quickly reach objectives and secure advantageous positions.

Most effective weapons:

  • Assault Rifles for medium range
  • Shotguns for close quarters
  • Sniper Rifles for long-distance encounters

Quick Tips

  • Purchase vehicles for faster travel around the map
  • Collect tokens regularly to unlock better items
  • Team up with others for better chances at airdrops
  • Check the leaderboard to track your progress
